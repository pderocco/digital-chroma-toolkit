voice: A software module capable of playing one note at a time. It has four pairs of control signal generators, including two glides, two sweeps, two basic envelopes, and two envelopes that can be delayed or triggered by things other than key presses. It also has four pairs of sound processing modules, including two oscillators, two waveshapers, two filters, and two amplifers, the last of which include output panners.

Note that the Voice Stack parameter allows up to four voices to be assigned to each note. They all have the same sound, but can be detuned and spread out in the stereo field.
