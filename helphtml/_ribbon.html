ribbon: An optional performance control that can be installed in the Digital Chroma. It consists of a Spectra Symbol SoftPot attached along the bottom edge of the front panel, with a hand-made cable that connects to the panel board inside the instrument. Part number SP-L-0400-203-3%-ST may be used on the left side, or SP-L-0500-203-3%-ST on the right side.
