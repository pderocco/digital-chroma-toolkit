player: A software module in the Digital Chroma that plays notes on a virtual {_instrument}. The keyboard is connected to two players, one for the main sound and the other for a {_link_program link} sound. There are also 64 players, one for each MIDI channel on the {_USB_device_port}, that are intended for sequence playback, when connected to a DAW.

When a {_program} is selected, the player creates a new "active" instrument, and sends all subsequent notes to it, but the previous instrument, now "inactive", continues to exist as long as any if its notes are held. {_parameter Parameter} changes are sent to the active instrument, so have no effect on inactive instruments. However, the player is where the {_performance_control} values are recorded, so they apply to both active and inactive instruments.
