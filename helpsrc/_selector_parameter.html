selector parameter: A {_parameter} that represents a set of discrete choices, such as a modulation source. In the Toolkit, it is represented as a set of {_button buttons}, one for each choice. On the Digital Chroma's control panel, it is treated like any other parameter, but the panel {_tapper} may be configured to tap as the {_parameter_control} goes from one setting to another.

Also: {_variable_parameter}
