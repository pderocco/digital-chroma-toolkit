tab: In the Toolkit, a label along the top of the Help {_screen} that contains the title of the help page being viewed. The Help screen can have multiple pages open, with multiple tabs along the top. A tab is selected by {_tap tapping} it, or closed by {_long-press long-pressing} it. If there are too many to fit, the row of tabs can be {_drag dragged} left or right. New tabs are created by long-pressing any help {_link_help link} or button.
