envelope: A {_control_signal} generator that generates a signal that is usually triggered at the start of a note, rises to a peak value that may vary with {_velocity} or other things, then optionally decay toward zero, and finally decay toward zero when the note is released. Each {_voice} has two basic envelopes, plus two more that can also be delayed or repetitively triggered by {_sweep} generators or other things. These latter two can also be computed by the virtual {_instrument}, for envelopes that are common to all {_voice voices}, for effects like simulating Hammond organ percussion.

Also: {_glide}, {_sweep}
