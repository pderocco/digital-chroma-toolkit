Powering off

Since the Digital Chroma is a computer, it's not generally a good idea to turn it off by yanking the power. This can, in rare cases, lead to corruption of the files that store your {_program programs}. Instead, press the power switch firmly for a second. The display will say "Shutting down\.", and the instrument will turn off after a second or so. Then, it is safe to unplug the unit if you need to pack it up and move it.

If the unit ever crashes (not that that is likely to happen), you can force the power to shut off by holding the power switch for five seconds. Then, after waiting a few seconds, you can press the power switch again to restart the instrument.
