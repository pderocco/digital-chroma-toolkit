latch: One of the functions of the left {_footswitch}, under control of the Footswitch Mode {_parameter}. In this mode, the footswitch performs a function similar to sostenuto in a piano, but more powerful. Pressing the footswitch "latches" the notes that are held, and the state of the sustain footswitch, for all voices currently sounding. Subsequently, the sustain footswitch can be released, and notes can be played completely independently of the latched notes. When the latch footswitch is released, the latched notes are no longer latched, but will continue to sound if the sustain footswitch is held.
