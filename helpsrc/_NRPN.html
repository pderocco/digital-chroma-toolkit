NRPN: In MIDI, a "Non-Registered Parameter Number", which is a composite control made up of three or four Control Change messages, providing access to a potentially huge number of parameters that are defined by an instrument's manufacturer and specific to an instrument. The Digital Chroma uses NRPNs to access {_parameter parameters}, perform {_program} operations, and invoke miscellaneous functions when communicating with the Toolkit.
