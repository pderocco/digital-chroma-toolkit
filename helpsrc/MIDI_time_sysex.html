MIDI time sysex

This contains six bytes which encode a UTC time and date, representing the current time. It is used by the {MIDI_Clock_Set Clock Set} {_NRPN}. The bytes are:

  • Year minus 2000 (0 to 99)
  • Month (1 to 12)
  • Day (1 to 31)
  • Hour (0 to 23)
  • Minute (0 to 59)
  • Second (0 to 59)

Illegal combinations, like February 29, 2019, are invalid.
