performance control: Any of the controllers built into, or plugged into, the Digital Chroma. This includes the two {_lever levers} to the left of the keyboard, the two {_pedal pedals} that may be plugged in, the VOLUME slider, the optional {_ribbon} controller, and the optional keyboard pressure sensor. In some contexts, it may include the dual {_footswitch}. It also includes any external controllers connected via MIDI.
