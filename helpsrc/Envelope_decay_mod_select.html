ENVELOPE decay mod select

These select one of twelve modulation sources for the decay time of the {_envelope} generators.

Toolkit

These are contained in the {Envelope_decay_cluster Envelope decay clusters} within the {Envelope_sections}.

Chroma panel

These are contained in the ENVELOPE 1 and ENVELOPE 2 sections of the right panel, under the following parameter numbers:

Env 1 Env 2
{sw-17.png height=48} {sw-24.png height=48}

These appear in the display as Env1/2 Dec Mod Select.

MIDI

These are accessed via the following {_NRPN NRPNs}:

Env 1 Env 2
A 1,19 1,32
B 2,19 2,32

MIDI display

1 +lever 2
