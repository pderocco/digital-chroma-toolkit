ENVELOPE decay self mod

These control the amount that the decay time of each {_envelope} generator is modulated by its own instantaneous value. This varies the shape of the decay curve from the usual simple exponential shape. Setting this to a negative value is particularly useful, because it can yield a much faster initial decay, and then only on those higher amplitude envelopes.

Toolkit

These are contained in the {Envelope_decay_cluster Envelope decay clusters} within the {Envelope_sections}.

Chroma panel

These are contained in the ENVELOPE 1 and ENVELOPE 2 sections of the right panel, under the following parameter numbers:

Env 1 Env 2
{sw-set-split.png height=48} {sw-16.png height=48} {sw-set-split.png height=48} {sw-23.png height=48}

These appear in the display as Env1/2 Dec Self Mod.

MIDI

These are accessed via the following {_NRPN NRPNs}:

Env 1 Env 2
A 1,18 1,31
B 2,18 2,31

Since these are {_bipolar} parameters, the values are offset by 64.
