footswitch: An on/off control operated by the foot. The Digital Chroma has a dual footswitch assembly in which the right footswitch is for sustain, and the left footswitch can be used as a latch (similar to sostenuto), or a gate, or a {_glide} enable. It also has a separate {_sequence_program} footswitch, which selects whatever {_program} is specified by the Sequence Program {_parameter} in the current program.

The Digital Chroma calls these controls "footswitches" since the term "pedal" is reserved for the variable {_pedal pedals} used for volume and expression.
