redo: The operation of undoing the last operation that was performed on the {_current_program}, or on a stored program. The Digital Chroma records 100 level of Undo, and of course has a corresponding Redo function. It uses the obsolete AUTO TUNE switch for Undo, and {_SET_SPLIT}, AUTO TUNE for Redo. The Toolkit uses the bottom two buttons in the {_Navigation_bar}.
