redo: The operation of undoing an {_undo}. After invoking the Undo function of the Digital Chroma, one can return to the present by invoking the Redo function the same number of times. The Digital Chroma uses the obsolete AUTO TUNE switch for Undo, and {_SET_SPLIT}, AUTO TUNE for Redo. The Toolkit uses the bottom two buttons in the {_Navigation_bar}.
