voice: A software module capable of playing one note at a time. It has four pairs of {_control_signal} generators, including two {_glide glides}, two {_sweep sweeps}, two basic {_envelope envelopes}, and two envelopes that can be delayed or triggered by things other than key presses. It also has four pairs of sound processing modules, including two {_oscillator oscillators}, two {_waveshaper waveshapers}, two {_filter filters}, and two {_amplifier amplifers}, the last of which include output panners.

Note that the Voice Stack {_parameter} allows up to four voices to be assigned to each note. They all have the same sound, but can be detuned and spread out in the stereo field.
