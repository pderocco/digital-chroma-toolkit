scratch flag: In the Digital Chroma's PROGRAM NUMBER display, an indicator that says "SCRATCH" when the {_current_program} doesn't exist. In the Toolkit, this is indicated by the {_Program_Info_button} showing the program number and name in yellow instead of light blue. All {_parameter parameters} have their default values. As soon as the sound is edited, a real program is created, and the {_modified_flag} appears instead.
