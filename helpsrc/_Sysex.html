Sysex: In MIDI, a "System Exclusive" message which carries an unlimited amount of bulk data in a format defined by an instrument manufacturer specific to its own instruments. The Digital Chroma uses a single Sysex message that carries bulk data, and is always immediately followed by an {_NRPN} that tells what it means, what to do with it, and what MIDI channel (if any) it belongs to.

Also: {_NRPN}, {_RPN}
