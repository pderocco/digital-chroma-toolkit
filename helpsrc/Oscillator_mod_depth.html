OSCILLATOR mod depth

These control the amount of pitch modulation. The displayed values are the number of semitones per {_unit} of modulation. There are five of these {_parameter parameters} for each {_oscillator}.

Toolkit

These are contained in the {Oscillator_mod_cluster Oscillator mod clusters} within the {Oscillator_section}.

Chroma panel

These are contained in the PITCH section of the right panel, under the following parameter numbers:

mod 1 mod 2 mod 3 mod 4 mod 5
{sw-28.png height=48} {sw-30.png height=48} {sw-32.png height=48} {sw-set-split.png height=48} {sw-30.png height=48} {sw-set-split.png height=48} {sw-32.png height=48}

These appear in the display as Osc Mod1-5 Depth.

MIDI

These are accessed via the following {_NRPN NRPNs}:

mod 1 mod 2 mod 3 mod 4 mod 5
A 1,38 1,41 1,43 1,45 1,47
B 2,38 2,41 2,43 2,45 2,47

Since these are {_bipolar} parameters, the values are offset by 64.
