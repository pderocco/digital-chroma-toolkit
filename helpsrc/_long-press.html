long-press: In the Toolkit, touching a {_control}, waiting at least half a second, and then releasing. While a regular {_tap} invokes the control's normal function, a long-press invokes a secondary function, such as resetting a {_parameter} to its default value, or opening a {_link_help Help link} in a new {_tab}.

Also: {_tap}, {_drag}, {_long-drag}
