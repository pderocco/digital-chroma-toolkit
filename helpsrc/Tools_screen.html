Tools screen

These are miscellaneous tools for various purposes:

{Old_Chroma_import} Importing old Chroma programs from the audio cassette format into Syntech {_Sysex} files.
{Old_Chroma_convert} Converting Syntech Sysex files into banks of Digital Chroma programs in the computer.
{Backup_restore} Backing up individual banks, or all banks, from the Digital Chroma to a folder, or restoring from a folder.
{Global_parameters} Adjusting the {_global_parameter global parameters} in the Digital Chroma.
