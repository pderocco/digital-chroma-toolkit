parameter control: The slider on the Digital Chroma's control panel that is used for adjusting most {_parameter parameters}. It is always connected to some parameter, but the user can connect it to any parameter by setting the {_panel_mode} to {_parameter_select_mode PARAM SELECT}, and then pressing a numbered switch, or by pressing {_SET_SPLIT} and a numbered switch to access the {_extended_parameter extended parameters} that are new in the Digital Chroma.
