AMPLIFIER mod depth

These adjust the amount of {_envelope} (or "fixed") signal that goes into each {_amplifier}. These two sources are added together, then multiplied by the {Amplifier_post_mod_cluster post mod} input.

Toolkit

These are contained in the {Amplifier_mod_cluster Amplifier mod clusters} within the {Amplifier_section}.

Chroma panel

These are contained in the VOLUME section of the right panel, under the following parameter numbers:

mod 1 mod 2
{sw-47.png height=48} {sw-49.png height=48}

They appear in the display as Amp Mod1/2 Depth.

MIDI

These are accessed via the following {_NRPN NRPNs}:

mod 1 mod 2
A 1,71 1,73
B 2,71 2,73
