pedal: One of the two variable pedals that can be plugged into the Digital Chroma. They are general-purpose controllers that can be used to modulate anything, although Pedal 1 is conventionally used for volume and Pedal 2 is used for other things.

Note that the Digital Chroma supports custom {_bipolar} pedals. With such a pedal, the normal "pedal" modulation sources only reflect the positive side of the pedal. To access the full bipolar range, use one of the "\+pedal" choices; to access just the negative side, use one of the "\-pedal" choices.

The Digital Chroma documentation refers to the switched controls like Sustain as "{_footswitch footswitches}" to avoid confusion.
