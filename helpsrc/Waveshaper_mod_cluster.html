WAVESHAPER mod cluster

{waveshaper-mod-cluster.png width=373}

This contains a standard {Waveshaper_mod_select mod select} {_parameter} and a corresponding {Waveshaper_mod_depth mod depth} {_slider}. Note that the depth on the slider is shown in cycles for the various FM {Waveshaper_shape shapes}, and in percent for the saws and pulse shapes. These have no effect if the shape is set to pink noise, white noise, or impulse.
