filter: A sound processing module that modifies the harmonic structure of a sound. Each voice has two, and each one can be tuned to any pitch and modulated by any {_control_signal control signals}. A filter can emphasize those frequencies around the pitch it is tuned to by turning its {_resonance} up, and usually attenuates frequencies above that point, but it can also attentuate frequencies below that point as well. Finally, if the resonance is turned all the way up, the filter becomes an oscillator, superimposing a sine wave on top of whatever signal is fed through the filter.

Also: {_oscillator}, {_waveshaper}, {_amplifier}
