glide: A {_control_signal} generator that takes the note pitches and turns them into smoothly sliding pitches (portamento) or rapid chromatic scales (glissando). Note that the A {_channel} glide generator always controls the A oscillator, and the B glide always controls the B oscillator, so if both are to glide together, the two glide generators must have the same {_parameter} settings.

Also: {_sweep}, {_envelope}
