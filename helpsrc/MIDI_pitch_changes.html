MIDI pitch changes

In normal MIDI, Note On and Note Off are supposed to be balanced. The Digital Chroma uses duplicate Note On messages preceded by Pitch LSB/MSB to change the pitch of a note without retriggering its envelopes.

Pitch LSB/MSB can also be used before a Note Off message, to change the pitch of the note as it is turned off, or before duplicate Note Off messages to change the pitch while it is dying out or held by the sustain footswitch. In these cases, the default pitch is its current pitch, not the pitch based on the note number.
