Toolkit

These are contained in the {Sweep_amplitude_cluster Sweep amplitude clusters} within the {Sweep_section}. One of the above diagrams is shown in the cluster popup.

Chroma panel

These are contained in the SWEEP section of the right panel, under the following parameter number:

{sw-11.png height=48}

These appear in the display as Sweep Shape.
