sweep: A {_control_signal} generator that generates a repetitive signal, usually called a "low frequency oscillator" or "LFO" in other instruments. Each {_voice} has two of them, and they can be used to modulate anything, although they are most commonly used for vibrato. Both the rate and amplitude of the sweep can be modulated by other things, such as {_lever levers} or {_envelope envelope}.

Sweeps can be synchronized to key presses. They can also be computed by the virtual {_instrument} instead of by each {_voice}, so that they are synchronous for all notes. Their rate can also be locked to received MIDI clocks.

Also: {_glide}, {_envelope}
