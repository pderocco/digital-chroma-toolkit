CONTROL pan spread

This controls how much stacked voices are spread out in the stereo field. This has no effect unless the {Control_voice_count} parameter is greater than 1.

This is expressed in degrees, representing the amount by which centered (mono) sounds are spread out by this parameter. When this is zero, all voices have the full pan range; when set to 90\d, the outer voices are panned half way to the sides, and their instruments each have half the panning range; when set to 180\d, the outer voices are forced all the way left and right, and the instrument pan controls do nothing.

Toolkit

This is contained in the {Control_voice_stack_cluster voice stack cluster} within the {Control_section}.

Chroma panel

This is contained in the VOLUME section of the right panel, under the following parameter number:

{sw-set-split.png height=48} {sw-49.png height=48}

This appears in the display as Pan Spread.

MIDI

This is accessed via {_NRPN} 0,30.
