global parameter: A numeric value that can be adjusted by the user, which controls some aspect of the overall operation of the Digital Chroma, and is not part of a {_program}. Examples are MIDI channel selection, and the {_lever} polarities. From the Digital Chroma's panel, they can be selected by pressing {_SET_SPLIT} and a number in the top row while in {_program_select_mode}, and then varied with the {_parameter_control}. In the Toolkit, they can be adjusted directly in the {Tools_screen}.
