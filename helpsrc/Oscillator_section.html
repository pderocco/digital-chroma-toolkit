OSCILLATOR section

{oscillator-section.png width=520}

The {_oscillator oscillators} generate audio frequencies, whose pitches are controlled by {_glide} generators, and whose shapes are generated by {_waveshaper waveshapers}. OSCILLATOR A is always controlled by GLIDE A, and its shape is produced by WAVESHAPER A, and the B side is similarly chained together. Each oscillator has the following {_parameter} {_cluster clusters}:

  • One {Oscillator_tune_cluster tune} cluster. All it has is one basic tune {_slider}.
  • Five {Oscillator_mod_cluster mod} clusters. Each {_cluster_button} shows the current mod select parameter, and the slider controls the mod depth. The first modulation cluster also has a {Oscillator_mod1_cluster steps} parameter, for sampling or quantizing.
