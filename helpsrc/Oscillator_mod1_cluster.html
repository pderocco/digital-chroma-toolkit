This contains a standard {Oscillator_mod_select mod select} {_parameter} and a corresponding {Oscillator_mod_depth mod depth} {_slider}. However, unlike the other four mod inputs, this one also includes a {Oscillator_mod_steps mod steps} parameter, to either introduce quantizing to the modulation, or to sample it by a {_sweep}.
