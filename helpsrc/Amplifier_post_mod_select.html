AMPLIFIER post mod select

These select one of 14 sources for post modulating each {_amplifier amplifier's} volume. They apply a reduction after the two primary sources (normally {_envelope envelopes}) are added together.

The "pedal" choices are useful for volume; pedal 1 should be preferred for that purpose, since it is at maximum value if the pedal isn't plugged in.

The "sweep" choices are useful for tremolo. Since most sweep shapes have a \+1 range, any depth over 50% will clip at zero.

The "pitch" choice is useful for balancing the sound across the keyboard, and works best with low (possibly negative) mod depths. See the {Amplifier_post_mod_depth mod depth} parameter for diagrams.

Toolkit

These are contained in the {Amplifier_post_mod_cluster Amplifier post mod clusters} within the {Amplifier_section}.
