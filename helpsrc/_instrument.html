instrument: A software process in the Digital Chroma that is capable of playing sounds controlled by a single {_program}. Every {_voice} belongs to the instrument that played it. When a new program is selected, a new instrument is created, so notes still sounding from the previous program are not affected.

While a voice does the sound generation for a single note, instruments do common processing, including tone control, distortion, and mono {_sweep} and {_envelope} generation.
