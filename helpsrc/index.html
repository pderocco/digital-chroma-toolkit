DIGITAL CHROMA TOOLKIT

The Toolkit runs on a laptop or desktop computer, and controls an attached Digital Chroma. It provides access to the {_current_program} controlling the sound, all the stored programs, and the {_global_parameter Global Parameters}.

The Toolkit window contains a {Navigation_bar} on the left, which is always visible, and a content screen on the right. The four middle buttons in the Navigation bar choose what screen is visible:

  • PROGRAMS displays a bank of {_program programs}, and allows you to select them for playing.
  • EDITOR displays the parameters in the current program, and allows them to be edited.
  • TOOLS provides conversion and backup, and access to the global parameters in the Digital Chroma.
  • HELP is what you're looking at now.

This Help screen provides documentation on the entire Digital Chroma Toolkit, and on the Digital Chroma itself. The Help browser's most important features are:

  • To scroll the page, drag it vertically with the left mouse button, or your finger on a touchscreen.
  • {_tap Tap} a blue link to follow it to another page. Tap a green link to pop up a definition, then tap the definition to dismiss it.
  • Tap the {back.png height=16} and {forward.png height=16} buttons at the top of the screen to go back or forward through your browsing history.
  • Tap a {question.png height=16} button anywhere to get context-sensitive help.
  • {_long-press Long-press} the HELP button to get back to this screen at any time.
