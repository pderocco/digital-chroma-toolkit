ENVELOPE decay mod depth

These control the amount that the decay time of each {_envelope} generator is modulated. The displayed values are the percentage of the total decay time parameter range covered by one {_unit} of modulation.

Toolkit

These are contained in the {Envelope_decay_cluster Envelope decay clusters} within the {Envelope_sections}.

Chroma panel

These are contained in the ENVELOPE 1 and ENVELOPE 2 sections of the right panel, under the following parameter numbers:

Env 1 Env 2
{sw-set-split.png height=48} {sw-17.png height=48} {sw-set-split.png height=48} {sw-24.png height=48}

These appear in the display as Env1/2 Dec Mod Depth.

MIDI

These are accessed via the following {_NRPN NRPNs}:

Env 1 Env 2
A 1,20 1,33
B 2,20 2,33

Since these are {_bipolar} parameters, the values are offset by 64.
